Recipe for choclate cake

    Ingredients:
  • Chocolate 300grams
  • Butter 100grams
  • Milk 200ml
  • 2 Eggs
  • Flour 400grams

How to make the choclate cake

  1. Melt the choclate & butter then mix it together
  2. Add milk to the batter
  3. Whisk the 2 eggs and add it to the batter
  4. sift the flour and it to the batter
